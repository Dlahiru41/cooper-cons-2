Company Overview

Established in 1996, Cooper Construction Shoring and Piling is a leading service provider of specialized Micro piling, sheet piling, shoring underpinning services with a rich legacy of delivering high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective solutions for construction and civil engineering projects.

With a team of skilled professionals, state-of-the-art equipment, and a commitment to safety and sustainability, we excel in providing support for a variety of construction projects, ranging from residential and commercial buildings to tunnels and large-scale infrastructure developments.

Our extensive experience and technical expertise enable us to tackle complex engineering challenges, ensuring the stability and safety of every project we undertake.
